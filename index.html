Who Should Take the Web & Mobile Development Program?

People Looking for a New Career

Web Developers are well paid, according to indeed Web Developers make on average of $60,000 a year in Utah. Most of our students leaving the program make about $16-$24 an hour, or between $33,000 to $49,000 a year upon graduation. Pay usually increases after about 1 year of experience.

Enterprenaurs or Freelancers

Whether you want to learn how to make a website for your company or you want to Freelance and make websites for others the Web & Mobile Development program can help you make your dream a reality. Many business owners don't have the budget to pay a Web Developer, the program will teach you how. As a Freelancer your income is only limited by how much you are willing to put into your business. Furthermore; there is nothing quite as rewarding as working on something that you have built.

Current developers looking to impove their skills

Are you currently employed as a junior or entree level developer and you want to improve your skills to get a better job? We can help you move beyond the basics so you can qualify for better employment.

Code hobbyist

It doesn't matter if you are interested in blogging or just want to learn how to code. We can teach you how to develop an online presence and learn to code for fun! Many of us who code find writing code to be enjoyable and even therapeutic.
